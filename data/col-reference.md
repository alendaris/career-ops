# Cost of Living — Relocation Reference

**Baseline:** Carrollton, TX (DFW) — $100,000–$120,000/year after-tax purchasing power target.  
**Texas income tax:** None. No state income tax.  
**Last updated:** 2026-05-19

---

## Methodology

Equivalent salary = Dallas target × COL multiplier × state income tax gross-up.

- **COL multiplier:** how much more expensive the target metro is vs. DFW (overall cost of living, not just housing)
- **Income tax gross-up:** additional pre-tax income needed to net the same after-tax dollars vs. Texas (no state income tax)
- **Purchasing power floor:** salary that lets James maintain the same lifestyle as $100K in Dallas
- **Purchasing power target:** salary that matches the Dallas $120K ceiling

## Markets: Confirmed Open to Relocation

### 1. Orlando / Lake Nona / Celebration / Bay Lake, FL

**Why:** Disney World proximity (DXT hub). FL has no state income tax — same as TX. Lake Nona is a growing analytics/healthcare tech cluster. Top relocation market for Disney roles.

| Factor | Value |
|--------|-------|
| COL vs. DFW | ~+10–18% |
| State income tax | None (FL) |
| Income tax gross-up | 1.0× (no adjustment needed) |
| Equivalent salary floor | ~$110,000 |
| Equivalent salary target | ~$142,000 |
| Sweet spot range | **$110,000–$142,000** |

**Notes:** Best COL match of all relocation markets. No income tax penalty. Greater Orlando (including Lake Nona, Celebration, Kissimmee) offers more affordable housing than Bay Lake/Disney resort areas directly.

## Scoring Guidance for Offer Evaluation

When scoring compensation (Block C) for roles in these markets, score against the equivalent salary range above — not the DFW baseline. A $130K offer in Orlando is equivalent to $115K in DFW (strong), while a $130K offer in Burbank CA is below purchasing power parity (score lower).

- **Do NOT penalize any of these markets for relocation** — James is explicitly open to all of them.
- **CA roles:** negotiate aggressively to the equivalent salary floor; Burbank Disney roles are at risk of falling short.
- **Orlando Disney roles:** best comp alignment; $123K–$165K posted range covers the equivalent target range well.
- **Toledo OH orbit:** score the specific employer city, not Toledo itself; Columbus/Ann Arbor roles score at DFW parity.
